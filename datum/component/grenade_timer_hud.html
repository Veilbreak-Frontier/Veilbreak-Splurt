grenade_timer_hud
/datum/component/grenade_timer_hud

Shows a live detonation countdown on the grenade's hand HUD icon. Visible to explosives specialists and to observers watching the holder. Spread into two parts: grenade_timer_hud is the inhand timer, grenade_timer_ground is the ground timer. The global manager centralizes countdown math; components handle their own visuals.
